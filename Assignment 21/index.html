Maths Formulae

  • Perimeter of square = 4 x side
  • Area of square = side x side
  • Total Surface Area of a Cube = 6 x side^2
  • Volume of a Cube = side^3


  • Perimeter of a rectangle = 2(length+breadth)
  • Are of a rectangle = length x breadth
  • Total Surface Area of a cuboid = 2(lb) + 2(bh) + 2(hl)
  • Volume of cuboid = length x breadth x height


  • Circumference of Circle = 2 x pi x radius
  • Area of a circle = pi x radius^2
  • Total Surface Area of Sphere = 4 x pi x radius^2
  • Volume of Sphere = 4/3 x pi x radius^3
